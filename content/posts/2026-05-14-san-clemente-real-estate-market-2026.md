# San Clemente Real Estate Market 2026 Outlook

The San Clemente real estate market is interesting right now. It's been slow, that's all. Not crashing, not booming. Slow.

## What I'm Seeing on the Ground

Inventory is tighter than buyers expect. Sellers who lived through the last few years still remember the frenzy, and some are waiting for it to come back.

I've been through a lot of cycles. The waiting game usually costs more than it saves.

## San Clemente Keeps Its Own Pace

People joke that San Clemente is the stepbrother of the beach towns. Maybe. But once you live here, nobody wants to leave.

That shows up in the numbers. Homes here hold their value because the supply of coastline doesn't grow.

## What This Means If You're Buying

A slow market is a negotiating market. You have room to ask for things buyers couldn't ask for three years ago.

You still need to move when the right house shows up. The good ones don't sit, even in a slow year.

## What This Means If You're Selling

Price it honestly from day one. Buyers down here are paying attention, and an overpriced listing just sits and gets stale.

I'd rather tell you the truth about your number than win the listing with a fantasy.
