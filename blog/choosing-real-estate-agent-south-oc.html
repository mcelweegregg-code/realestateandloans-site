How to Choose the Right Agent in South Orange County

Not all agents know this market the same way. Picking a real estate agent in South Orange County is probably the biggest hiring decision most families ever make, and most people spend less time on it than they spend picking a dishwasher.

Ask How Long They've Worked This Coast

Experience isn't everything, but it tells you a thing or two. An agent who has been through a few cycles knows what a slow market actually looks like.

I've been doing this for almost 40 years. I've seen the booms, the busts, and everything between.

Ask What They Actually Specialize In

Probate, divorce, first-time buyers, luxury. These are different jobs. A divorce sale needs someone neutral who knows what they're doing, because both parties are going through one of the hardest seasons of their lives.

If an agent says they do everything equally well, keep asking questions.
