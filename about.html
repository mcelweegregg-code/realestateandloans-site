What nearly 40 years on one coast actually means

Gregg McElwee has been selling real estate along the South Orange County coast since 1987. Nearly four decades in a single market means he has seen every cycle, every shift, and most of the streets. He knows which neighborhoods hold value when the market softens, which ones move fast when it heats up, and what it actually takes to get a great deal on a listing or top dollar on a sale. That is not something you read in a report. It is something you accumulate, year by year, deal by deal.

Most agents handle standard transactions. Gregg handles those too, but a meaningful part of his practice runs through the harder ones, probate sales, divorce proceedings, estate transitions. These are situations where a court may be involved, where emotions run high, where timing matters and mistakes are costly. Gregg has spent years working through exactly these kinds of sales. He knows the process, he knows the parties typically involved, and he knows how to keep a complicated transaction moving forward without adding to an already difficult situation.

One thing you will get with me is a personal relationship. In the end, that goes a long way.

Gregg is a current San Clemente resident with deep connections across the area. He is not working a territory from the outside, he lives here, he knows the neighbors, and he has a stake in the community. If you are thinking about buying, selling, or navigating a more complex real estate situation anywhere along the South OC coast, he would welcome the conversation.
